E-commerce Site

E-commerce, short for electronic commerce, is the process of conducting business transactions online through websites or mobile apps. It includes activities like buying and selling products, booking services, and making online payments. E-commerce platforms allow businesses to operate beyond physical boundaries and provide customers with the flexibility to shop anytime and from anywhere. These platforms typically include features like product listings, shopping carts, secure checkout systems, and customer reviews. With the rise of digital payment systems, fast delivery services, and digital marketing, e-commerce has become an essential part of the modern economy. It benefits both businesses and customers by offering cost efficiency, convenience, and wider market reach.

Form Processing

Form processing is a fundamental part of web development that allows users to input and submit data through interactive fields on a webpage. It typically involves collecting user inputs such as name, email, password, and other details via HTML forms, and then handling this data using backend technologies like PHP, JavaScript, or databases. When a user submits a form, the data is validated (checked for correctness), processed (stored or acted upon), and often followed by feedback to the user (like a success message). Form processing is widely used in features like user registration, login systems, contact forms, feedback submissions, and online orders. It plays a vital role in dynamic websites by making them interactive and user-responsive.
